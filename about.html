About Parisian Apartments

Your trusted choice for luxurious, short-term rental apartments in the heart of Paris.

Who We Are

At Parisian Apartments, we are passionate about offering travelers a unique, comfortable, and unforgettable experience in the City of Light. Our carefully curated collection of luxurious apartments allows you to immerse yourself in the vibrant culture of Paris, while enjoying the comforts of home.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to provide premium short-term rental experiences that combine style, convenience, and personalized service. Whether you're visiting Paris for a romantic getaway, a family vacation, or a business trip, we have the perfect space for you to relax and enjoy.

Why Choose Us?

  • Prime locations in the heart of Paris, close to popular attractions like the Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, and Champs-Élysées.
  • Handpicked, stylish apartments with luxurious amenities, offering the ultimate Parisian experience.
  • Flexible booking options for both short and extended stays.
  • Personalized concierge service to help you make the most of your visit.
  • 24/7 customer support to assist you during your stay.

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Parisian Apartments was born from a love for Paris and a desire to share its magic with visitors from around the world. What started as a small collection of boutique apartments has grown into a diverse portfolio of unique, luxurious homes that offer guests a truly local experience.

With years of experience in the short-term rental industry, we’ve established a reputation for excellence and attention to detail. Our team works tirelessly to ensure that every guest feels welcome and cared for during their stay, making Parisian Apartments the top choice for discerning travelers.
